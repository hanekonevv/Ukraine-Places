Places to visit in Ukraine


Truskavets is a city in the Drohobych district of the Lviv region of Ukraine. Administrative center of the Truskavets city community. Balneological resort, formerly a resort of all-Union significance. Located in the foothills of the Ukrainian Carpathians, at an altitude of 400 m, south 84 km from Lviv, 9 km from Drohobych.

The fairy-tale city of Kamianets Podilskyi nestles in the western Ukrainian countryside and has gained its fame with tourists owing to the Kamianets-Podilskyi Fortress. The beauty of this placelies in its cobbled streets. lined with beautiful pastel-colored houses and street murals depicting the history of the city. .

The Carpathian Mountains span Central Europe, and Ukraine has a small portion of this magnificent mountain. Feel delighted visiting the lush woodland and unspoiled scenery of this place. The presence of pine forests adds to the beauty of the place.
